WHO IS PINOCCHIOP?

pinocchiop, sometimes abbreviated by fans as pinop, is an INCREDIBLE vocaloid producer with sooo many highly successful songs and an instantly recognizable style !!!! hes literally been dropping bangers as long as i have been alive ouuuughh, as his first song is from 2009. he usually makes songs with hatsune miku but has also used kagamine rin, kagamine len, and kasane teto.
his tuning style has changed over the years, as in his earlier songs miku has a much harsher and deeper tone. now, his miku is usually very soft and sweet sounding! both are so beautiful and distinctively pinop!! you can also usually hear his own voice in the background singing with miku, a wonderful signature of his style!!
